Early life

  • Bhagat Singh was born into a Punjabi Sikh family on 27 September 1907 in the village of Banga
  • After being sent to the village school in Banga for a few years, Bhagat Singh was enrolled in the Dayanand Anglo-Vedic School in Lahore
  • In 1923, he joined the National College in Lahore

      • Revolutionary activities

      • Killing of John Saunders
      • Killing of Channan Singh
      • Escape from Lahore
      • Delhi Assembly bombing and arrest
